About NIAD

NIAD was never created to be perfect.

It lives in the space between raw, unfinished, and beautifully real. We believe beauty exists in imperfection—where irregular forms, unexpected details, and handmade textures create true character.

Every piece is intentionally imperfect, designed to feel authentic rather than polished. NIAD embraces individuality, quiet boldness, and self-expression for those who find beauty in the undone and the uncommon.
